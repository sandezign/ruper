# Rumper Product Roadmap

**Status:** Active hypothesis
**Roadmap horizon:** Approximately 15-18 months at 10 hours per week
**Review cadence:** Monthly
**Current phase:** Phase 1 - Validation MVP

## Roadmap Purpose

This roadmap translates Rumper's [product vision](../vision/product-vision.md) and [product strategy](../strategy/product-strategy.md) into five evidence-based product phases.

Dates are indicative planning windows, not automatic launch commitments. Rumper advances only when the current phase's exit gate is met.

## Strategic Alignment

**Vision:**

> Every homebuyer in Indonesia can make a confident property decision based on independent and understandable location-risk evidence.

**Strategic choice:**

> Win the pre-DP decision moment for first-time homebuyer couples through trusted, personalized concierge audits before automating delivery.

**North Star Metric:**

> Number of home-purchase decisions meaningfully supported by Rumper.

## Roadmap Principles

1. Validate paid decision value before building software.
2. Automate only proven, repeated operational bottlenecks.
3. Preserve buyer-side independence and evidence quality at every phase.
4. Treat customer decision impact as more important than report volume.
5. Progress based on evidence, not elapsed time.

## Phase Summary

| Phase | Indicative window | Product form | Primary outcome | Exit decision |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **1. [Validation MVP](phase-1-validation-mvp.md)** | Month 0-1 | Manual paid beta audit | Prove buyers will pay and use Rumper before commitment | Launch, iterate, or pause |
| **2. [Concierge MVP](phase-2-concierge-mvp.md)** | Months 2-4 | Repeatable paid audit service | Prove repeatable acquisition, delivery, and decision value | Optimize, automate selectively, or pause |
| **3. [Semi-Automation](phase-3-semi-automation.md)** | Months 5-8 | Human-reviewed internal workflow | Improve capacity and margin without reducing trust | Productize, continue service model, or pause |
| **4. [Assisted Self-Service](phase-4-assisted-self-service.md)** | Months 9-13 | Customer self-service intake with human-reviewed output | Prove software improves conversion and customer experience | Scale product, revise, or retain assisted model |
| **5. [Scale and B2B Exploration](phase-5-scale-and-b2b.md)** | Months 14-18+ | Scalable B2C product with selected partner pilots | Prove scalable distribution and recurring intelligence value | Scale, focus, or reject expansion |

## Phase 1: Validation MVP

**Indicative window:** Month 0-1
**Product form:** Manual mini-checks and paid beta location-risk audits
**Primary question:** Will qualified buyers pay for and use Rumper before a real booking-fee or down-payment decision?

Detailed execution plan: [Phase 1 Plan](phase-1-validation-mvp.md)

### Entry Criteria

- Product positioning and initial beachhead segment are defined.
- A basic research, report, and delivery workflow can be operated manually.
- Rumper can recruit buyers with a real shortlisted property.

### Customer Outcome

Buyers understand the most important risks of a shortlisted location and know what to validate before committing.

### Business Outcome

Demonstrate paid demand, decision impact, and initial delivery feasibility.

### MVP Scope

- Short customer intake and qualification.
- Manual location-risk research.
- Single-location beta audit.
- Dual-location comparison test.
- Confidence-labeled findings.
- Practical recommendation and survey checklist.
- PDF or shareable report delivered through WhatsApp.
- Customer feedback and decision-impact follow-up.

### Core Deliverables

- Intake form and qualification criteria.
- Mobile-readable mini-check and audit report templates.
- Research, scoring, quality-control, and delivery checklists.
- Validation tracker.
- Customer interview and WhatsApp scripts.
- First paid beta reports and case studies.

### Success Metrics

| Metric | Phase target |
|---|---:|
| Target-customer conversations | 10-15 |
| Qualified leads | At least 10 |
| Real property locations submitted | At least 5 |
| Mini-checks or beta reports delivered | 5-10 |
| Paid beta customers | 2-3 minimum; 3-5 preferred |
| Customers rating the report above 8/10 | At least 80% |
| Paid customers reporting a changed or validated action | At least 60% |
| Average Comprehensive report production time | Below 2.5 hours |

### Exit Gate

Advance to Phase 2 when:

- Qualified buyers demonstrate real paid behavior.
- Reports influence or validate customer actions.
- Customers understand and trust the report.
- A repeatable manual delivery workflow is possible.
- At least one segment, pain, message, and acquisition path shows credible demand.

Otherwise choose `iterate` or `pause` using the [30-Day Pre-Launch Validation Plan](../../business/validation/30-day-pre-launch-validation-plan.md).

### Out of Scope

- Customer accounts.
- Automated scoring or report generation.
- Interactive maps.
- Payment integration.
- Broad paid-acquisition campaigns.

## Phase 2: Concierge MVP

**Indicative window:** Months 2-4
**Product form:** Formal paid concierge audit service
**Primary question:** Can Rumper repeatedly acquire qualified buyers and deliver trusted reports with viable early economics?

Detailed execution plan: [Phase 2 Plan](phase-2-concierge-mvp.md)

### Entry Criteria

- Phase 1 exit gate is met.
- Beachhead segment, primary pain, offer, and message are selected.
- Report workflow and quality standards are documented.

### Customer Outcome

Qualified buyers consistently receive useful, evidence-based decision support within a predictable delivery period.

### Business Outcome

Establish repeatable paid acquisition, reliable delivery, and an economically credible core offer.

### Product Scope

- Comprehensive single-location audit.
- Dual-location comparison package.
- Standardized intake, research, scoring, and quality control.
- Formal pricing and payment process.
- Public sample report and trust proof.
- Customer consultation and follow-up.
- Referral and second-location purchase flows.

### Core Deliverables

- Launch-ready offer and package definitions.
- Standard operating procedures for the complete order lifecycle.
- Standard report and comparison templates.
- Source registry and reusable evidence library.
- Funnel, production-time, quality, and unit-economics dashboards.
- Testimonials and case studies.

### Success Metrics

| Metric | Phase target |
|---|---:|
| Paid Comprehensive or Dual-Location reports | At least 10 |
| Customer acquisition cost | Below Rp150,000 |
| Average order value | At least Rp300,000 |
| Customers rating the report above 8/10 | At least 80% |
| Paid customers reporting a changed or validated action | At least 60% |
| Average Comprehensive report production time | Below 2.5 hours |
| Repeat, second-location, or referral signal | Demonstrated by multiple customers |

### Exit Gate

Advance to Phase 3 when:

- At least one acquisition path produces qualified paid buyers repeatedly.
- Customer decision impact and satisfaction remain strong.
- Delivery quality is stable.
- Unit economics have a credible path to positive contribution margin.
- Repetitive production bottlenecks are measured and clearly identified.

### Out of Scope

- Customer-facing automated recommendations.
- Expansion outside Jabodetabek.
- Native mobile application.
- Partner dashboard or public API.

## Phase 3: Semi-Automation

**Indicative window:** Months 5-8
**Product form:** Human-reviewed reports supported by internal automation
**Primary question:** Can Rumper increase capacity and improve margin without reducing trust or decision value?

Detailed execution plan: [Phase 3 Plan](phase-3-semi-automation.md)

### Entry Criteria

- Phase 2 exit gate is met.
- Repeated workflows and bottlenecks are supported by production data.
- Report quality and evidence standards are stable.

### Customer Outcome

Buyers receive consistent reports faster while retaining personalized findings, transparent evidence, and human judgment.

### Business Outcome

Reduce production time and cost per report while increasing the number of decisions supported per operating hour.

### Product Scope

- Structured internal research records.
- Reusable location evidence library.
- Standardized scoring guidance.
- Internal report generator.
- Internal order and production dashboard.
- Assisted quality-control checks.
- Semi-automated payment and customer communication.

### Core Deliverables

- Structured location and evidence schema.
- Internal analyst workflow.
- Reusable report components.
- Automated repetitive calculations and report assembly.
- Production analytics by workflow stage.
- Human-review and escalation rules.

### Success Metrics

| Metric | Phase target |
|---|---:|
| Median report production time | Below 90 minutes, trending toward 60 minutes |
| On-time delivery rate | At least 90% |
| Customer satisfaction above 8/10 | At least 80% |
| Decision-impact rate | At least 60% |
| Evidence reuse on eligible reports | Increasing month over month |
| Contribution margin per report | Positive or clearly improving |
| Quality-control failure rate | Stable or decreasing |

### Exit Gate

Advance to Phase 4 when:

- Automation measurably improves a proven bottleneck.
- Quality, trust, satisfaction, and decision impact do not decline.
- Structured data supports consistent personalized analysis.
- Customer-facing self-service can solve a measured acquisition or intake problem.

### Out of Scope

- Fully automated final recommendations.
- Removing human review from material risk findings.
- Building software features without a measured workflow problem.
- Nationwide geographic expansion.

## Phase 4: Assisted Self-Service

**Indicative window:** Months 9-13
**Product form:** Customer self-service intake and preview with human-reviewed final output
**Primary question:** Can software improve customer conversion, speed, and experience while preserving trusted analysis?

Detailed execution plan: [Phase 4 Plan](phase-4-assisted-self-service.md)

### Entry Criteria

- Phase 3 exit gate is met.
- Customer intake, qualification, and report workflows are stable.
- Semi-automation has improved internal delivery.
- A clear customer-facing problem justifies productization.

### Customer Outcome

Buyers can easily submit a property, explain their priorities, pay, track progress, and receive useful initial guidance before the reviewed report arrives.

### Business Outcome

Increase conversion and delivery capacity while reducing manual coordination and maintaining decision value.

### Product Scope

- Self-service location and buyer-priority intake.
- Automated qualification and package recommendation.
- Initial risk preview with clear limitations.
- Online payment.
- Order-status communication.
- Human-reviewed final report.
- Customer report archive.
- Assisted comparison of multiple locations.

### Core Deliverables

- Customer-facing web application v0.
- Secure order and payment workflow.
- Buyer-priority profile.
- Initial risk-preview experience.
- Admin review and approval workflow.
- Report archive and follow-up system.
- Product analytics and customer-support workflow.

### Success Metrics

| Metric | Phase target |
|---|---:|
| Qualified lead-to-paid conversion | At least 10-20% |
| Paid reports per month | 8-15 within founder capacity |
| Median production time | Below 60 minutes |
| On-time delivery rate | At least 95% |
| Customer satisfaction above 8/10 | At least 80% |
| Decision-impact rate | At least 60% |
| Manual coordination time per order | Meaningfully reduced |
| Contribution margin | Positive and improving |

### Exit Gate

Advance to Phase 5 when:

- Self-service improves conversion or customer experience.
- Assisted delivery supports higher volume with stable quality.
- Acquisition economics and contribution margin support careful scaling.
- The evidence library and structured workflows create reusable value.

### Out of Scope

- Fully automated high-confidence purchase recommendations.
- Native mobile application without proven web demand.
- Nationwide rollout.
- Partner products that compromise buyer-side independence.

## Phase 5: Scale and B2B Exploration

**Indicative window:** Months 14-18+
**Product form:** Scalable assisted B2C product with selected partner and intelligence pilots
**Primary question:** Can Rumper scale trusted distribution and create recurring value from its location-risk intelligence?

Detailed execution plan: [Phase 5 Plan](phase-5-scale-and-b2b.md)

### Entry Criteria

- Phase 4 exit gate is met.
- B2C acquisition and delivery economics are credible.
- Product quality and decision impact remain stable at increased volume.
- Rumper has reusable location-risk evidence and clear partner hypotheses.

### Customer Outcome

More buyers can access trusted location-risk support, directly or through buyer-aligned advisors, without sacrificing independence or clarity.

### Business Outcome

Establish scalable acquisition and test recurring partner or data-product revenue.

### Product Scope

- Expanded multi-location comparison.
- Scalable content, search, referral, and partner acquisition.
- Area-level evidence and insight products.
- Buyer-aligned partner referral workflow.
- Limited partner dashboard or structured export pilot.
- Selective geographic expansion based on data readiness and demand.

### Core Deliverables

- Scaled B2C acquisition playbook.
- Multi-location comparison experience.
- Partner pilot framework and independence policy.
- Area-risk intelligence prototype.
- Partner dashboard or export prototype.
- Geographic expansion readiness checklist.

### Success Metrics

| Metric | Phase target |
|---|---:|
| Monthly paid B2C volume | Sustained growth with viable economics |
| Primary acquisition channel | At least one scalable channel identified |
| Customer satisfaction above 8/10 | At least 80% |
| Decision-impact rate | At least 60% |
| Contribution margin | Positive and stable |
| Buyer-aligned partner pilots | 1-3 validated pilots |
| Partner repeat usage or willingness to pay | Demonstrated |
| Geographic expansion readiness | Evidence-based, not assumed |

### Exit Gate

Choose the next strategic focus:

- **Scale B2C:** when direct customer acquisition and product economics are strongest.
- **Develop partner distribution:** when buyer-aligned partners show recurring demand.
- **Develop intelligence products:** when reusable evidence creates measurable recurring value.
- **Focus:** when expansion weakens independence, economics, or customer decision impact.

### Out of Scope Until Separately Validated

- Seller-funded recommendations.
- Broad developer partnerships that compromise independence.
- Open public API.
- Nationwide expansion without reliable data and operational readiness.
- Unreviewed automated final recommendations.

## Cross-Phase Capability Development

| Capability | Phase 1 | Phase 2 | Phase 3 | Phase 4 | Phase 5 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Customer understanding | Discover urgent pain | Confirm repeatable segment | Personalize consistently | Encode into intake | Expand carefully |
| Research and evidence | Manual research | Standardized SOP | Structured and reusable | Product-integrated | Area-level intelligence |
| Report delivery | Manual beta PDF | Repeatable paid service | Assisted generation | Human-reviewed self-service | Scalable assisted delivery |
| Acquisition | Founder-led outreach | Repeatable trust channel | Measured channel economics | Product conversion | Scalable and partner channels |
| Data | Validation tracker | Evidence library | Structured location data | Customer and product data | Intelligence products |
| Defensibility | Independence | Trust and decision impact | Operational knowledge | Workflow and data reuse | Distribution and recurring intelligence |

## Roadmap Governance

### Monthly Review

Review:

- North Star and current phase metrics.
- Customer decision-impact evidence.
- Qualified funnel and paid behavior.
- Production time, quality, and contribution margin.
- Assumptions accepted, rejected, or still uncertain.
- Whether the current phase exit gate has been met.

### Phase Decision

At the end of each phase, record one decision:

- `advance`
- `iterate`
- `pause`
- `focus`
- `stop`

Document the evidence and consequences in [`product/decisions`](../decisions/README.md).
