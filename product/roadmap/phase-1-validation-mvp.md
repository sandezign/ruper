# Phase 1 Plan: Validation MVP

**Duration:** 4 weeks
**Capacity:** 40 gross hours; 32 planned hours; 8-hour buffer
**Primary owner:** Founder
**Phase goal:** Prove that qualified first-time homebuyers will submit a real location, pay for independent analysis, and use the report before commitment.

## Phase Constraints

- Maximum planned work: 8 hours per week.
- Maximum concurrent reports: 2.
- Maximum active experiments: 2.
- No software development beyond simple forms, trackers, and templates.
- The existing [30-Day Pre-Launch Validation Plan](../../business/validation/30-day-pre-launch-validation-plan.md) remains the detailed experiment source of truth.

## Weekly Capacity Model

| Workstream | Hours/week |
|---|---:|
| Customer recruitment and interviews | 2 |
| Report production and quality control | 3 |
| Offer, pricing, and message experiments | 1 |
| Feedback and decision-impact follow-up | 1 |
| Metrics review and process improvement | 1 |
| Unplanned buffer | 2 |

## Workstreams

### Customer Discovery

- Recruit buyers who have a real shortlisted property and expect to decide within three months.
- Capture the buyer's decision timeline, strongest concern, alternatives, and current research behavior.
- Prioritize the Almost-DP Couple segment.

### Product Validation

- Deliver a limited mini-check that demonstrates evidence quality without replacing the paid audit.
- Sell and deliver paid beta single-location audits.
- Test a dual-location comparison with relevant buyers.
- Observe whether users understand the recommendation, confidence labels, and next actions.

### Operational Validation

- Measure time spent on research, scoring, writing, quality control, delivery, and follow-up.
- Record evidence gaps and recurring research problems.
- Improve the report template after every delivery.

### Commercial Validation

- Ask for actual payment before producing full reports.
- Test the approved beta price ranges.
- Record rejection reasons verbatim.

## Weekly Plan

### Week 1: Prepare and Recruit

**Outcome:** Rumper can recruit and qualify buyers without building additional product.

| Activity | Hours |
|---|---:|
| Finalize intake, tracker, scripts, and beta offer | 3 |
| Direct outreach and community recruitment | 3 |
| Conduct first interviews | 2 |

**Deliverables:**

- Approved intake and qualification flow.
- Validation tracker.
- Mobile-readable mini-check template.
- At least five target-customer conversations.
- Three to five qualified real locations.

### Week 2: Deliver and Observe

**Outcome:** Buyers can understand and use the initial report.

| Activity | Hours |
|---|---:|
| Produce and quality-check three mini-checks | 5 |
| Observe comprehension and collect feedback | 2 |
| Revise report template | 1 |

**Deliverables:**

- Three delivered mini-checks.
- Production-time baseline.
- Recorded comprehension and usability findings.
- Revised report template.

### Week 3: Test Paid Behavior

**Outcome:** Rumper has credible willingness-to-pay evidence.

| Activity | Hours |
|---|---:|
| Offer paid beta audits and record objections | 2 |
| Produce paid or additional beta reports | 5 |
| Request testimonials and referrals | 1 |

**Deliverables:**

- Two to three paid beta customers minimum.
- Complete rejection-reason log.
- At least one strong testimonial or case-study permission.

### Week 4: Consolidate and Decide

**Outcome:** Rumper has enough evidence to launch, iterate, or pause.

| Activity | Hours |
|---|---:|
| Complete feedback and decision-impact follow-up | 2 |
| Analyze funnel, customer language, and economics | 2 |
| Update report, SOP, scoring, and offer | 2 |
| Write phase decision record | 2 |

**Deliverables:**

- Phase scorecard.
- Selected beachhead segment, pain, message, offer, and channel.
- Updated operating workflow.
- Formal `advance`, `iterate`, or `pause` decision.

## Critical Path

```text
Qualified buyer recruitment
→ Real location submission
→ Useful mini-check
→ Paid audit purchase
→ Delivered report
→ Decision-impact follow-up
→ Phase decision
```

## Exit Scorecard

| Evidence | Required threshold |
|---|---:|
| Qualified leads | At least 10 |
| Real locations submitted | At least 5 |
| Paid beta customers | At least 2-3 |
| Satisfaction above 8/10 | At least 80% |
| Changed or validated customer action | At least 60% of paid customers |
| Average Comprehensive production time | Below 2.5 hours |

## Risks and Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Leads are interested but not near a real decision | Enforce qualification criteria and prioritize decision timeline |
| Free mini-check removes willingness to pay | Keep scope limited and offer paid audit immediately |
| Reports consume all weekly capacity | Limit concurrent reports to two and narrow report scope |
| Buyers praise the report but take no action | Follow up after the real decision deadline |
| Founder changes too many variables at once | Run no more than two active experiments |

## Phase Review Checklist

- [ ] All customer and operational metrics are recorded.
- [ ] Paid behavior is separated from stated interest.
- [ ] Decision-impact follow-up is complete.
- [ ] Strongest segment, pain, message, offer, and channel are documented.
- [ ] Report-production bottlenecks are ranked.
- [ ] Phase decision and evidence are recorded.
