# Rumper Product Vision

**Status:** Active
**Review cadence:** Annual, or after a fundamental market change

## Selected Vision

> Every homebuyer in Indonesia can make a confident property decision based on independent and understandable location-risk evidence.

This vision describes the customer future Rumper wants to create. It does not commit Rumper to a particular product format, technology, or business model.

## Why This Vision

Buying a home is a high-value and difficult-to-reverse decision. First-time buyers often make that decision using fragmented information, biased sales claims, short surveys, and advice that is difficult to verify.

Rumper exists to make independent location-risk evidence understandable and useful at the moment buyers still have the power to investigate, compare, negotiate, delay, or walk away.

The selected vision is:

- **Inspiring:** it aims to improve the quality and confidence of home-buying decisions across Indonesia.
- **Achievable:** Rumper can begin with one decision moment, one segment, and one geography before expanding.
- **Emotional:** it addresses fear of regret and the desire to make a responsible decision for oneself and one's family.

## Product Mission

> Help first-time homebuyers identify, understand, and validate location risks before committing financially to a home.

The mission defines Rumper's present role. The initial commitment moment is before a booking fee or down payment.

## Customer Future State

When Rumper succeeds:

- Buyers can distinguish verified evidence from marketing claims and uncertain signals.
- Buyers understand which location risks matter for their own lives.
- Buyers know what to validate during a physical survey.
- Couples and families can discuss shortlisted homes using a shared, evidence-based framework.
- Buyers can proceed, investigate further, compare alternatives, or walk away with greater confidence.

## Product Principles

1. **Buyer-first and independent.** Rumper serves the buyer's decision and does not optimize for closing a property transaction.
2. **Evidence over claims.** Important findings show their source, recency, confidence, and limitations.
3. **Decision support over data overload.** Rumper translates fragmented information into prioritized findings and next actions.
4. **Personalized risk relevance.** Risk importance reflects the buyer's household, commute, priorities, and tolerance.
5. **Transparent uncertainty.** Rumper separates verified facts, analyst judgment, and items requiring field validation.
6. **Calm, not fear-based.** Rumper explains risk clearly without exaggeration or unsupported guarantees.
7. **Field-validation friendly.** Rumper complements physical surveys by helping buyers know what to inspect and ask.

## Customer-Facing Expression

**Brand promise:**

> Lebih yakin sebelum DP.

**Category explanation:**

> Cek risiko lokasi rumah sebelum booking fee atau DP.

**Primary campaign message:**

> Rumahnya bagus. Risikonya sudah dicek?

These expressions communicate the vision in simple customer language without promising that Rumper can eliminate all risk.

## Vision Boundaries

The vision does not mean Rumper must:

- Become a property marketplace or sales agent.
- Guarantee that a property is safe, profitable, or free from future problems.
- Replace legal due diligence, building inspection, or physical surveys.
- Recommend properties based on sales commission.
- Cover every property risk or Indonesian city immediately.

## Evidence of Progress

Rumper is moving toward the vision when:

- Buyers use Rumper evidence before real booking-fee or down-payment decisions.
- Rumper changes or validates a buyer's next action.
- Buyers can explain important risks and evidence gaps to their family.
- More decisions can be supported without reducing trust, clarity, or evidence quality.

## Vision Options Considered

1. **Selected:** Every homebuyer in Indonesia can make a confident property decision based on independent and understandable location-risk evidence.
2. Make hidden location risks visible before they become lifelong regrets.
3. Give every Indonesian homebuyer an independent second opinion before commitment.
4. Build a future where no family chooses a home without understanding the location risks.

The selected vision best balances broad customer impact, independence, evidence, and long-term credibility.
