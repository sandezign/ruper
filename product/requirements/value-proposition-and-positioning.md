# Rumper Value Proposition and Brand Positioning

## Product Context

| Item | Definition |
|---|---|
| Product | Rumper |
| Category | PropTech, location-risk intelligence, and GIS-powered residential due diligence |
| Primary market | First-time homebuyers in Jabodetabek |
| Core product | Independent location-risk audit before the buyer pays a booking fee or down payment |
| Primary output | PDF report covering location score, flood risk, commute, road access, amenities, red flags, and a survey checklist |

Rumper's role is to protect the buyer before commitment. It is a buyer-side decision-support service, not a property marketplace, sales agent, home inspector, or generic GIS tool.

## Customer Profile

### Primary Customer

First-time homebuyers in Jabodetabek who have shortlisted one to three homes and are close to paying a booking fee or down payment.

They are typically:

- Buying through a KPR or using long-term savings
- Worried about making an expensive, irreversible mistake
- Looking for objective evidence to discuss with a spouse, parents, or family
- Skeptical of claims made by agents, developers, listings, and brochures
- Short on the time or expertise needed to investigate scattered location data

### Main Personas

**Almost-DP Couple:** A young couple who are excited about their first home but financially anxious. They want reassurance and evidence before committing.

**Commuter First Buyer:** An office worker considering an affordable home in a satellite city who needs to understand the real daily commute, road access, and livability.

## Customer Jobs

### Functional Jobs

- Evaluate whether a location is safe and livable
- Check flood risk and verify claims such as `bebas banjir`
- Understand realistic daily commute time and effort
- Compare one to three property candidates objectively
- Identify hidden location red flags before making a payment
- Prepare better questions for a physical survey
- Make a confident proceed, reconsider, or avoid decision

### Emotional Jobs

- Feel safe before making a major financial commitment
- Reduce anxiety and the risk of regret after moving in
- Resist pressure from agents and limited-time promotions
- Feel confident that proper due diligence has been completed

### Social Jobs

- Show family members that the decision is rational and evidence-based
- Reach agreement when family members use different evaluation criteria
- Demonstrate responsibility when making a large purchase

## Customer Pains

### High-Priority Pains

1. **Property information is biased.** Sellers emphasize benefits and rarely disclose flood history, poor access, noise, pollution, or commute problems.
2. **Marketing claims are difficult to verify.** Buyers lack an independent way to check claims such as `strategis`, `dekat stasiun`, or `hanya 15 menit ke Jakarta`.
3. **Location data is fragmented.** Relevant signals are spread across maps, news, social media, government data, forums, videos, and local residents.
4. **Commute reality is unclear.** Map distance can hide traffic, poor station access, weak public transport, and difficult first-mile or last-mile travel.
5. **Wrong decisions are expensive.** A poor location can cause repair costs, higher transport costs, reduced resale value, stress, and lower quality of life.

### Supporting Pains

- Buyers lack a standard framework for comparing houses
- Buyers do not know which questions to ask local residents
- Families may disagree because they evaluate options differently
- Important problems may only become visible after moving in

## Customer Gains

### Expected Gains

- A clear view of whether the location's risks are acceptable
- Independent analysis of flood, commute, access, and environmental risks
- A readable report that saves time compared with manual research
- A more objective way to compare properties

### Desired Gains

- A simple score or risk label for each location
- A clear proceed, caution, verify further, or avoid recommendation
- Evidence that can be discussed with a spouse or family
- A physical-survey checklist
- Guidance on which risks are serious and which are manageable

### Delight Opportunities

- Reveal hidden risks the buyer did not notice
- Include relevant signals from local community discussions
- Show realistic commute scenarios by transport mode
- Explain the confidence level of each finding
- Help the buyer negotiate, investigate further, or cancel before losing more money
- Compare two locations side by side

## Brand Positioning Strategy

### Brand Territory

**Rumper is the buyer's decision protector before down payment.**

Rumper protects first-time homebuyers from making rushed, poorly informed property decisions. It helps buyers pause, examine location risks objectively, and decide based on evidence before making a major financial commitment.

The brand territory combines:

- **Emotional promise:** protection from regret and greater confidence before committing
- **Functional category:** independent home-location risk checking
- **Decision moment:** before paying a booking fee or down payment

Rumper should consistently own the moment immediately before commitment. This makes the brand more emotionally relevant than a generic property-research service and more accessible than a technical due-diligence platform.

### Core Positioning Statement

> For first-time homebuyers who are afraid of making the wrong decision, Rumper is an independent service that checks location risks before booking fee or down payment, so they can buy with greater confidence and less regret.

### Brand Promise

**Lebih yakin sebelum DP.**

This promise should be used as Rumper's short, memorable expression of customer value. It communicates the desired emotional outcome without claiming that Rumper can eliminate all risk.

### Campaign Positioning

**Primary campaign message:**

> Rumahnya bagus. Risikonya sudah dicek?

**Supporting messages:**

- Brosur menunjukkan kelebihan. Rumper membantu melihat risikonya.
- Rumah bisa direnovasi. Lokasi sulit diubah.
- Jangan biarkan tekanan sales menentukan keputusan besar.
- Cek banjir, commute, akses jalan, dan red flag lokasi sebelum DP.
- Keputusan besar membutuhkan bukti, bukan hanya janji.

### Messaging Hierarchy

| Level | Message | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Brand promise | Lebih yakin sebelum DP. | Build emotional relevance and memorability |
| Category | Cek risiko lokasi rumah | Explain what Rumper does in simple language |
| Core benefit | Membantu melindungi pembeli dari keputusan rumah yang buruk | Connect the service to a meaningful outcome |
| Functional proof | Cek banjir, commute, akses jalan, dan red flag lokasi | Make the offer concrete |
| Trust proof | Independen, berpihak pada pembeli, dan berbasis bukti | Reduce skepticism |
| Action | Cek dulu sebelum booking fee atau DP | Create urgency around the decision moment |

### Communication System

Rumper should use a combination of firm preventive hooks and calm, objective guidance.

| Communication stage | Tone | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Awareness hook | Firm, concise, and preventive | Interrupt assumptions and highlight overlooked risk |
| Educational content | Simple, practical, and non-judgmental | Help buyers understand what and how to check |
| Product explanation | Clear and evidence-based | Show how Rumper reduces blind spots |
| Report and recommendation | Calm, transparent, and objective | Support a considered decision without creating panic |
| Conversion message | Direct and time-sensitive | Encourage checking before commitment |

### Brand Voice Principles

- **Protect, do not frighten.** Explain consequences clearly without exaggerating risk.
- **Challenge claims, not people.** Verify developer or agent claims without portraying every seller as dishonest.
- **Translate evidence into decisions.** Avoid technical GIS language when a practical explanation is sufficient.
- **Be decisive about findings and transparent about uncertainty.** Clearly separate facts, interpretation, and evidence gaps.
- **Respect the buyer's agency.** Rumper informs the decision; the buyer remains the decision-maker.

### Positioning Pillars

#### 1. Buyer-Side Independence

Rumper has no incentive to close the property transaction. Its role is to help the buyer evaluate whether the location is suitable and whether identified risks are acceptable.

**Proof points:**

- Independent from agents and developers
- Analysis designed around buyer needs
- Findings include negative signals and evidence gaps

#### 2. Protection Before Commitment

Rumper is most valuable before the buyer pays a booking fee or down payment, when there is still time to investigate further, negotiate, compare alternatives, or walk away.

**Proof points:**

- Pre-DP risk audit
- Proceed, caution, verify further, or avoid recommendation
- Survey checklist tailored to identified risks

#### 3. Evidence Over Marketing Claims

Rumper helps buyers test claims such as `bebas banjir`, `strategis`, `dekat stasiun`, and `akses mudah` using available data and practical location analysis.

**Proof points:**

- Evidence screenshots and references
- Confidence labels based on data quality
- Clear separation between verified findings and items requiring field checks

#### 4. Practical Decision Clarity

Rumper converts fragmented location information into a clear report that buyers can understand and discuss with their spouse or family.

**Proof points:**

- Simple risk score and labels
- Prioritized findings rather than raw data
- Actionable final recommendation

### Positioning Guardrails

Rumper should not position itself as:

- A guarantee that a location is safe, profitable, or free from future problems
- A replacement for a physical property survey, legal due diligence, or building inspection
- A fear-based media brand that sensationalizes every potential risk
- A generic map, listing portal, or technical GIS platform
- An agent that recommends properties based on sales commission

All brand communications should reinforce Rumper's role as an independent decision-support service that reduces blind spots before commitment.

### Value Proposition Statements

**Consumer:** Rumper helps first-time homebuyers check hidden location risks, including flood, commute, road access, and environmental red flags, before paying a booking fee or down payment.

**Strategic:** Rumper is an independent residential location-risk intelligence service that converts scattered geospatial and local signals into a simple proceed, caution, or avoid recommendation for homebuyers.

**Investor:** Rumper is building a buyer-side property due-diligence layer for Indonesia, starting with concierge location-risk audits and evolving into a GIS-powered residential risk-scoring platform.

**One-line:** Rumper helps first-time homebuyers avoid bad property decisions by auditing hidden location risks before they pay a booking fee or down payment.

## Positioning Risks and Guardrails

| Risk | Guardrail |
|---|---|
| The report covers too many dimensions for an early MVP | Prioritize flood, commute, road access, red flags, and the final recommendation |
| Manual analysis becomes too slow as demand grows | Measure production time and standardize repeatable research steps |
| Public data is limited or outdated in some areas | Display confidence labels and clearly identify evidence gaps |
| Recommendations sound like guarantees | Separate facts, analyst judgment, and limitations; never guarantee safety or investment performance |
| Buyers prefer free research | Demonstrate unique findings, time saved, and the value of avoiding a costly decision |
