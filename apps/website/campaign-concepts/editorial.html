04 · FAQ

Questions before you begin.

Does Rumper sell homes?

No. Rumper is not a property agent and does not sell home listings.

Does Rumper guarantee the location is risk-free?

No. Rumper provides risk indicators based on available data and does not replace a physical site visit.

Who is it for?

First-time homebuyers with a shortlisted home in Greater Jakarta.
